Namaste, my name is Yugal bihari ji. I was born in Shri Dham Vrindavan, India. Vrindavan is considered one of the holiest places in India, as it was the site where Lord Krishna spent his childhood days. Being from a Brahmin family, Vedic literature, religious ceremonies, and spiritual guidance have been a part of our lives for decades. I have a passion for travelling and have always found happiness and peace. .
